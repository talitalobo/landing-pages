Party hard, lost hard

You are in India's Holi Festival, surrounded by hundreds of people and everyone is completely covered with paint. So ... where's your roommate? Your Indian date girl?
Find Your Friends allows you to easily locate your friends and family using your smartphone. =]

It's easy and simple!

Install FYF on your Android smatphone, sign in with your Google Account and you are ready! View your location, add friends, create groups ...

Try FYF now :D

FYF is available in Play Store of Google, or here. It's free!
